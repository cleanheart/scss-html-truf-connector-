Pricing and Why It Is Worth the Investment

The cost of installing artificial turf typically starts at around $7 per square foot, with variations depending on the type of turf and the complexity of the installation. Several factors contribute to the cost:

Quality of Materials

Higher-grade materials that look more natural and last longer are more expensive. Also, depending on the amount of rock needed for the bases layer, labor for applying the rock is expensive.

Labor

Professional installation ensures a durable and visually appealing finish but requires skilled labor, which can be a significant part of the cost. This is either done with equipment (which most companies rent) or manually with a wheelbarrow and steel rake. Also, accessibility to your project area could plays a role in pricing. For instance, if equipment cannot fit inside the gate to your backyard and there are steps leading to your project area, the manual labor will be more expensive to wheelbarrow and carry the rock to your project.

Preparation Work

The extent of ground preparation needed can affect the overall price.
